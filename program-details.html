Pathway to Renewal.

Revival Summit 2026

Theme: The Renewal of the Oath
Date: August 7–8, 2026
Venue: International Conference Centre, University of Ibadan

Purpose of the Summit

Return to covenant faithfulness

Repent, renew, and recommit

Consecrate your life afresh to God

Embrace revival as a lifestyle, not an event

Through Scripture-centered teaching, guided consecration, prayer, worship, and intergenerational engagement, participants will be led to renew their oath, their sacred commitment to Christ and His purposes.

Who Should Participate

This is not a conference for spectators. It is a moment of decision.

  • If you are hungry for authentic revival…
  • If you desire depth over noise…
  • If you long to walk faithfully in your generation…

This Summit is for you.
