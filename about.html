About The Summit

Called by God. Prepared for you.

The Revival Summit is a sacred gathering born out of a deep longing for spiritual renewal, covenant alignment, and generational restoration. It exists to call believers back to the heart of God, beyond religion, routines, and nostalgia, into a living, obedient, and transformative walk with Him.

At its core, the Revival Summit is about renewal of the oath: a return to the foundational commitments of faith, holiness, compassion, and obedience to Christ. In a time when Christianity is often reduced to culture, convenience, or emotional experience, this summit creates space for sincere repentance, biblical reflection, and fresh surrender to God’s purposes.

The Summit brings together seasoned elders, ministry leaders, and a rising generation of believers who hunger for authentic revival, not as an event, but as a lifestyle. Through prayer, teaching, worship, dialogue, and intergenerational engagement, participants are challenged to rediscover what it truly means to be born again, Spirit-led, and mission-focused in today’s world.

Revival Summit is not about hype. It is about heart work. It is a call to reawaken spiritual responsibility, restore forgotten truths, and raise a generation anchored in conviction, compassion, and Christlike leadership ready to carry revival into families, communities, and nations.

Our Vision.

To see a generation of believers restored to authentic faith rooted in Scripture, awakened by the Spirit, and committed to living out the covenant of Christ with integrity, compassion, and courage. We envision revival not as a momentary gathering, but as a sustained movement of transformed lives influencing families, churches, communities, and nations.

Our Mission.

The Revival Summit (August 7 – 8, 2026) exists to facilitate The Renewal of the Oath, a deliberate return to the foundational commitments of the Christian faith. Our mission is to:

  • Call believers back to covenant faithfulness through repentance, reflection, and renewed obedience to Christ
  • Bridge generations, creating space for elders to impart wisdom and testimony while equipping the rising generation with clarity, conviction, and spiritual responsibility
  • Restore biblical understanding of revival, moving beyond emotional experiences to lasting spiritual transformation
  • Awaken a missional consciousness, reminding believers that true revival produces compassion, justice, and sacrificial service
  • Equip participants to carry the fire of renewal beyond the summit, into daily life, leadership, and community impact

The Revival Summit is intentionally designed as a sacred pause, a time to realign hearts, renew vows, and recommit to God’s original calling. What begins at the Summit is meant to continue as a lifelong pursuit of holiness, humility, and Spirit-led living.
